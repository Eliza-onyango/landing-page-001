About Us

Smart Pest Deterrent System tackles the menace of quelea birds and locusts, offering a comprehensive solution integrating cutting-edge technologies. Our real-time monitoring using drones and AI-powered pest identification ensure accurate and efficient pest management, prioritizing environmental sustainability with acoustic deterrents. Designed for scalability, our system empowers farmers of all sizes. With a focus on precision pest control and real-time monitoring, Smart Pest Deterrent System represents a transformative shift in agricultural pest management, safeguarding food security and the environment.
